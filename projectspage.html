Frequently Asked Questions

What is the role of the Project Management Office (PMO) in our organization?

The PMO provides governance, standards, and support for managing projects across the organization. We ensure that projects align with strategic goals, monitor progress, manage risks, and provide tools, templates, and training to enhance project delivery.
